Who We Are

At Backslider Performance, we build cars that are fun to drive and uniquely exciting.

With years of experience in performance tuning, race prep, custom fabrication, and electronic integration, we push every build to its absolute limit. From track-focused race cars and EV conversions to daily drivers with serious bite, we engineer performance that's built to dominate, down to every sensor, signal, and weld.

No cutting corners, no "good enough," no builds that fall apart after their first hard pull. Every project is a collaboration between our expertise and your vision, and we don't call it done until it's something you can beat on with confidence and grin about every time you turn the key.
